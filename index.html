About Me

Hi im Jorelle, but you can call me JoJo for short.

I am a motivated and passionate 24 year old game programmer and gamer, based in Cabuyao, Laguna, Philippines.

I have a strong affinity for planning, programming, and developing games from the ground up, and ever since I got introduced to Game Dev as a specialization subject in college I never stopped learning.

I started learning Unity as it was the best tool for beginners aswell as having a very tight community. Learning how to make a game, with my background in C++, C#, and Java, had its challenges but all in all it was a fun experience with a ton of lessons learned and it led me to making my very first game aswell as my capstone project.
